The story

The U.S. and Iran have been locked in a conflict that defies easy categories — not quite war, never quite peace. It began when Iranian students stormed the U.S. Embassy in 1979. It has since included hostage crises, proxy armies, computer viruses, assassinations, crippling sanctions, a nuclear deal, and direct missile exchanges.
